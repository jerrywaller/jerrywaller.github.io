There's this great tool called CircuitLab that lets you design and test circuits in your web browser. Because I'd seen someone use supercapacitors as a makeshift UPS (uninterruptible power supply) on a Raspberry Pi, I knew I had to try my hand at designing one of my own. The trick to supplying power to small linux development boards like these is that they eat up a lot of current–one to two amps, as a matter of fact–but at a relatively low voltage (5V).
